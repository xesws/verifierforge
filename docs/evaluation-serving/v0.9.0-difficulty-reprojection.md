# v0.9.0 — B1/B3 evaluation evidence

B1 is a reference-mode, pod-local-vLLM probe over exactly 326 records: 276
accepted candidates plus 50 Git-bound original seeds. It writes one atomic
per-prompt JSONL count (`0..8`) and normal Gate A evidence; neither is an
admission result. B2 consumes only those count records and the immutable probe
population.

B3 is the sole admission rerun over the projected 50 rows, at `k=8` and with
v2 sample evidence enabled. Its pass@1, pass@8, and mixed fraction remain
unmodified Gate A metrics; no adaptive threshold or sampling policy is allowed.
