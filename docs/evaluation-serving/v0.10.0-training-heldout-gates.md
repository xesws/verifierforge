# v0.10.0 — U2 Gate A and held-out measurement boundary

Gate A is fixed to `k=8` in gate mode. Its evidence records three required
predicates: pass@1 range `[0.20, 0.60]`, mixed fraction minimum `0.30`, and
pass@8 minimum `0.85`. A missing or non-eight `k` cannot produce an admission
pass; reference-mode probes remain descriptive.

The selected 50-row pool is evaluated only as an admission/training-monitoring
surface. The new 60-row held-out set receives pre-training baseline and
post-training/DoD evaluations; no training-pool metric may be used to claim
improvement. Both surfaces retain verifier/config/input hashes and sample
evidence according to their evidence policy.
