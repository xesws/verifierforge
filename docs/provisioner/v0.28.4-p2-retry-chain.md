# v0.28.4 — immediate-previous retry chain

A replacement has two identities: the immutable approval root and the
immediately preceding terminal attempt. Admission requires the approval to
remain bound to the root, `training.retry-created.approval_root` to match it,
and `training.retry-admitted.previous_external_id` to equal that event's
`retry_of`. Terminal/deletion audits must match the immediately preceding pod.

The next job suffix increments mechanically to `-r4`. Earlier conservative
spend is carried from admission evidence and the `-r3` elapsed interval is
added before the provider create. Gold/orphan remain skipped.
