## Problem and scope

The v0.40.0 Technical Deep Dive PDF was incorrectly published under
`output/pdf/`. It is a judge-facing submission artifact and therefore belongs
directly under `docs/submission/` with the other DevPost materials.

Move the PDF and checksum without changing their bytes, update the exporter,
README, version records, and regression test, and remove the now-empty
`output/` tree.

## Explicit non-goals

- No PDF content, layout, evidence, or website change.
- No regeneration unless byte identity unexpectedly changes.
